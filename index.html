Johnny MyungWon Lee

Edinburgh, United Kingdom

I am a PhD student in the School of Mathematics at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Dr. Daniel Paulin and Dr. Miguel de Caravalho. I am part of the Statistics research group. I am currently funded by the School of Mathematics PhD Studentships

My primary research interests lie in the field of Bayesian statistics, meta learning, machine learning and statistical computation, and they share the same goal of discovering insights through data. I am also increasingly passionate about the intersection of machine learning and our daily lives, including algorithms that draw inspiration from mathematics, finance, natural language processing that use machine learning to accelerate scientific discovery.
