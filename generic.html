More About Me

The most enthusiastic person you will ever meet.

My name is Johnny MyungWon Lee, (My given name is pronounced as M'yung-won Lee). I was born in Korea but lived in Singapore for 15 years. I came to the United Kingdom in 2016 to study in the University of Edinburgh. I graduated with a Bachelor in Science in Mathematics and Statistics with a First Class Honours degree at the University of Edinburgh in 2022. At this current stage, I am a doctoral researcher at the School of Mathematic of the University of Edinburgh funded by the School of Mathematics PhD Studentships.

I am an enthusiastic person who is eager to learn and an experienced researcher who demostrated active involvment in the field of Bayesian Statistics and Machine Learning. I was mesmerised by the powerful performance of Bayesian Statistics in my final year of study during my Undergraduate. Even though it was researched by many others from the 80's, I still see the great potential to expand in various aspect such as Variational Inference.

Like any others, I am starting this blog to share any information that could benefit anyone who shares common interest. Although my posts will be narrowed down to programmings, statistics (Especially Bayesian Statistics) and machine learning, I wish that my posts could bring interest and food for thoughts.
